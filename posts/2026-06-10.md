As a single parent, you're managing it all — and you deserve to know where you really stand financially. 📊 That's what net worth tells you: add up everything you own (savings, your car, your home) and subtract everything you owe (credit cards, loans, debt). Whatever's left is your number.

Don't panic if it's small or even negative right now — most people start there. What matters is the direction it's moving. Pay down a little debt, save a little more, and watch that number grow month after month. 💪

That number is proof of the life you're building for your kids. 🌱
