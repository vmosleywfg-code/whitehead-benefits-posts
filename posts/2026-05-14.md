You built a business — but do you know the difference between what's building you up and what's dragging you down? 💼 Assets put money IN your pocket: cash, equipment you own, real estate, business revenue, investments. Liabilities take money OUT: loans, credit card balances, unpaid invoices you owe. Most business owners focus on sales but never look at whether their net worth is actually growing. Here's the rule: grow your assets, shrink your liabilities.
