# Life Insurance Post — May 1, 2026

**Topic:** The Hidden Value of Insuring a Stay-at-Home Parent

---

🏡 Here's something many families overlook: **stay-at-home parents need life insurance too.**

It's easy to assume that life insurance is only for the household breadwinner — but think about everything a stay-at-home parent actually does. Childcare, cooking, transportation, household management, tutoring, scheduling… If something tragic happened, replacing all of those services would cost a family tens of thousands of dollars a year.

According to industry estimates, the economic value of a stay-at-home parent's work can exceed $100,000 annually when you add it all up. 💛 Yet millions of families have no coverage in place for the person holding it all together at home.

Life insurance for a stay-at-home parent means your family wouldn't have to scramble to cover daycare, afterschool care, or other household needs during an already devastating time. It's not about income replacement — it's about **role replacement**, and that has real, measurable value.

The good news? Coverage for stay-at-home parents is often very affordable. Starting a conversation now — before you ever need it — is one of the most loving things you can do for your family. ❤️
