# Facebook Post — Why Your Work Life Insurance May Not Be Enough

💼 Your job offers life insurance — that's great! But here's what many people don't realize: it's probably not enough to truly protect your family.

Most employer-provided life insurance covers just 1–2 times your annual salary. Financial experts generally recommend 10–12 times your income. That gap can leave your loved ones struggling to cover a mortgage, childcare, and daily expenses for years to come.

There's another risk: if you leave your job — or lose it — that coverage disappears. Your family's protection shouldn't depend on your employment status. 💛

An individual policy stays with *you*, not your employer — and locking in coverage while you're young and healthy means lower premiums for life. 🛡️
