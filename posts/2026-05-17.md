So you've decided you want life insurance — great first step. 💡 But now you're staring at "term" and "whole life" wondering what they even mean.

**Term life** covers you for a set period — 20 or 30 years — at a lower monthly cost. Perfect while you're building your career, paying off debt, and laying your foundation.

**Whole life** covers you for life AND builds cash value over time that you can actually borrow from. 💰 Think of it as protection plus a financial tool in one.
